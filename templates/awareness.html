Treatment Options

💉 Cancer Therapies

🧪 Chemotherapy
Effects
  • Kills rapidly dividing cancer cells
  • Shrinks tumors before/after surgery
  • Helps stop or slow spread
Side Effects
  • Hair loss
  • Nausea
  • Fatigue
☢ Radiation Therapy
Effects
  • Destroys tumor cells with targeted energy
  • Precisely targets a specific area
Side Effects
  • Skin irritation
  • Fatigue
🧬 Immunotherapy
Effects
  • Boosts the immune system's response
  • Helps the body fight cancer naturally
Side Effects
  • Fever
  • Inflammation
