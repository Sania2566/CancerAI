Cancer Awareness

Early Detection Saves Lives

Knowledge is your most powerful weapon. Understand the signs, risk factors, and preventive measures for the most common types of cancer.

🎀

Breast Cancer

1 in 8 women will be diagnosed with breast cancer. Monthly self-exams and annual mammograms after 40 are essential for early detection.

🫁

Lung Cancer

Lung cancer is the leading cause of cancer death. Quit smoking, avoid secondhand smoke, and test your home for radon to reduce your risk significantly.

🩺

Colorectal Cancer

Regular colonoscopies starting at age 45 can catch and remove precancerous polyps early. A healthy diet high in fiber dramatically lowers risk.

☀️

Skin Cancer

The most common cancer globally. Applying SPF 30+ daily, wearing protective clothing, and getting yearly skin checks can reduce your risk by 80%.
